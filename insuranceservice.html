Life Insurance

Covers risk of death or survival — protects family financially

🛡️
Term Insurance

Pure protection plan with payout only on death during the policy term

  • ✓ Lowest premium, highest coverage
  • ✓ No maturity benefit
  • ✓ Best for young families
♾️
Whole Life Insurance

Covers entire lifetime (usually up to 99-100 years)

  • ✓ Death benefit + some savings
  • ✓ Lifetime coverage
  • ✓ Higher premium than term
💰
Endowment Plan

Insurance + Savings - Pays sum assured on death or at maturity

  • ✓ Dual benefit plan
  • ✓ Guaranteed maturity benefit
  • ✓ Savings component included
💵
Money Back Plan

Periodic payouts during the policy term with remaining amount paid on maturity

  • ✓ Regular income during policy
  • ✓ Survival benefits
  • ✓ Life cover continues
📈
ULIP

Insurance + Investment in market-linked funds for dual benefit

  • ✓ Protection + wealth creation
  • ✓ Market-linked returns
  • ✓ Flexible investment options
👶
Child Plan

For child's education or marriage with future benefits even if parent dies

  • ✓ Secures child's future
  • ✓ Premium waiver on death
  • ✓ Education milestones covered
🧓
Pension / Retirement Plan

Builds corpus for retirement with regular pension after maturity

  • ✓ Retirement income
  • ✓ Tax benefits available
  • ✓ Guaranteed pension options
💊

Senior Citizen Health Plan

  • ✓ Higher sum insured options
  • ✓ Special senior benefits

Maternity Health Plan
