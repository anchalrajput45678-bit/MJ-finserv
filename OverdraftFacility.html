Professional Overdraft Facility

Flexible credit line for professionals to manage cash flow fluctuations and unexpected expenses with ease.

What is a Professional Overdraft?

A professional overdraft is a flexible credit facility that allows qualified professionals to withdraw more money than they have in their account, up to an approved limit. It's designed to help you manage cash flow fluctuations, unexpected expenses, and business opportunities without the need for a formal loan application each time.

Interest is charged only on the amount utilized, not the entire limit

Key Benefits

  • Flexible access to funds up to your approved limit
  • Pay interest only on the amount you use
  • No collateral required for limits up to $100,000
  • Instant access through internet banking and cheques
  • Renewable annually with minimal documentation

Higher Limits

Overdraft limits up to $200,000 for eligible professionals based on income and qualifications.

Competitive Rates

Interest rates starting from 10.5% p.a. with no hidden charges or processing fees.

Quick Approval

Get approval within 48 hours with minimal documentation for qualified professionals.

Overdraft Eligibility

Our professional overdraft facility is available to a wide range of qualified professionals meeting the following criteria:

Qualification

Minimum professional degree in medicine, engineering, law, finance, or other recognized fields.

Income

Minimum annual income of $75,000 for salaried professionals or $50,000 for self-employed.

Experience

Minimum 2 years of professional experience in your field of qualification.

Credit History

Good credit score with no major defaults or delinquencies in the past 12 months.
