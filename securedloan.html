Check Eligibility

Verify your eligibility based on age, income, credit score, and property/asset valuation. Most secured loans have flexible eligibility criteria.

Submit Documents

Provide KYC documents, income proof, property papers, and any loan-specific documents. Digital submission available for faster processing.

Property/Asset Valuation

Bank/lender will evaluate your collateral. For property loans, a technical and legal verification is conducted by approved valuers.

4
