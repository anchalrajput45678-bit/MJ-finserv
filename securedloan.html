Secured vs Unsecured Loans

Understanding the key differences to make the right choice

🔐 Secured Loans

  • ✓ Backed by collateral (property, gold, vehicle)
  • ✓ Lower interest rates (8-12% typically)
  • ✓ Higher loan amounts available
  • ✓ Longer repayment tenure (up to 30 years)
  • ✓ Easier approval with collateral
  • ✓ Better for large expenses

📝 Unsecured Loans

  • ✓ No collateral required
  • ✓ Higher interest rates (12-24%)
  • ✓ Lower loan amounts
  • ✓ Shorter tenure (1-5 years typically)
  • ✓ Credit score based approval
  • ✓ Quick processing

How to Get a Secured Loan?

Simple 5-step process to get your loan approved

Choose Your Loan Type

Select the secured loan that matches your collateral and financial needs. Consider factors like interest rate, tenure, and loan amount.

1

Check Eligibility

Verify your eligibility based on age, income, credit score, and property/asset valuation. Most secured loans have flexible eligibility criteria.

2

Submit Documents

Provide KYC documents, income proof, property papers, and any loan-specific documents. Digital submission available for faster processing.

3

Property/Asset Valuation

Bank/lender will evaluate your collateral. For property loans, a technical and legal verification is conducted by approved valuers.

4

Loan Disbursal

Once approved, loan amount is disbursed to your account. Typically takes 7-15 days from application for property-backed loans.
