Business Loan FAQs

Find answers to common questions about our business loan products and application process.

What documents do I need to apply for a business loan?

Typically, you'll need business financial statements (profit & loss, balance sheet), business and personal tax returns, business legal documents (articles of incorporation, licenses), and personal identification. The exact requirements may vary based on the loan type and amount.

How long does the approval process take?

For most business loans, we provide initial approval decisions within 24-48 hours of receiving a complete application. Once approved, funding can be available in as little as 3-5 business days, depending on the loan type and documentation requirements.

What is the minimum credit score required?

While we consider each application holistically, most of our business loans require a minimum personal credit score of 650. However, we have specialized programs for businesses with lower credit scores or newer businesses with limited credit history.

Can I pay off my business loan early?

Yes, most of our business loans allow for early repayment without prepayment penalties. This gives you the flexibility to pay off your loan ahead of schedule if your business experiences strong cash flow.
