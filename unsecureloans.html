‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

What is an unsecured loan? +
An unsecured loan is a loan that doesn't require any collateral or security. It's approved based on your creditworthiness, income, and repayment capacity. Your assets remain safe even if you default, though it may affect your credit score.
How quickly can I get an unsecured loan? +
Most unsecured loans can be approved within 24-48 hours. Some lenders offer instant approval and same-day disbursal, especially for personal loans and salary loans with proper documentation.
What documents are required? +
Basic documents include: Identity proof (Aadhaar, PAN), Address proof, Income proof (salary slips/ITR), Bank statements (3-6 months), and Employment proof. Additional documents may be required based on loan type.
What is the minimum credit score needed? +
Most lenders prefer a credit score of 750 or above for unsecured loans. However, some lenders may approve loans with scores as low as 650-700, but with higher interest rates.
Can I prepay my unsecured loan? +
Yes, most unsecured loans allow prepayment. However, some lenders may charge a prepayment penalty (usually 2-5% of the outstanding amount). Check your loan agreement for specific terms.
