Frequently Asked Questions

What is Overseas Life Insurance? +

Overseas Life Insurance provides financial protection worldwide, ensuring your family receives a payout if you pass away ‚Äî even while living, working, or travelling outside your home country. You can purchase it from Indian insurers (if you're an NRI or OCI) or from international insurance companies if you live/work abroad long-term.

Who should consider Overseas Life Insurance? +

Overseas Life Insurance is ideal for:

  • Indian citizens working abroad (NRIs)
  • Indian students or professionals living overseas for years
  • Foreign residents working in India or multiple countries
  • Business owners with global travel exposure

What documents are required for NRIs/Expats? +

Typical documentation includes:

  • Passport & Visa copy
  • Address proof (Indian + overseas)
  • Income proof / Employment contract
  • Health declaration or medical test report
  • NRI status declaration (for Indian insurers)

Can I transfer my existing Indian policy if I move abroad? +

Most Indian life insurance policies continue to provide coverage even if you move abroad, though you should inform your insurer about your change in residence. Some insurers offer specific NRI policies with enhanced global coverage. It's best to check with your insurance provider about policy portability and any additional requirements for overseas coverage.
