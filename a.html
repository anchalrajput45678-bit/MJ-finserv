Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about unsecured loans

What is an unsecured loan? +
An unsecured loan is a loan that doesn't require any collateral or security. It's approved based on your creditworthiness, income, and repayment capacity. Your assets remain safe even if you default, though it may affect your credit score.
How quickly can I get an unsecured loan? +
Most unsecured loans can be approved within 24-48 hours. Some lenders offer instant approval and same-day disbursal, especially for personal loans and salary loans with proper documentation.
