Personal Loan FAQs

Find answers to common questions about our personal loan products and application process.

What is the minimum credit score required for a personal loan?

While we consider each application holistically, most of our personal loans require a minimum credit score of 620. However, we have specialized programs for applicants with lower scores or limited credit history. Your income, employment stability, and debt-to-income ratio are also important factors in the approval decision.

How long does it take to get approved and receive funds?

You'll receive an instant preliminary decision after completing our online application. Once you've submitted all required documents, final approval typically takes 1-2 business days. After approval, funds are usually deposited into your bank account within 1-3 business days, making the entire process from application to funding as quick as 2-5 business days.

Are there any fees associated with personal loans?

We pride ourselves on transparency. Our personal loans have no origination fees, no application fees, and no prepayment penalties. The only costs you'll pay are the interest on your loan and, if applicable, late payment fees if you miss a payment. All costs are clearly disclosed before you accept the loan offer.

Can I use a personal loan for debt consolidation?

Yes, debt consolidation is one of the most common uses for our personal loans. By consolidating multiple high-interest debts (like credit cards) into a single personal loan with a lower interest rate, you can simplify your monthly payments, potentially reduce your total interest costs, and pay off your debt faster. We can even help you pay your creditors directly.
